THE CRAFT

Making kulfis is not a business pursuit for us, but an art that we have mastered over the years. Using only the finest and freshest ingredients, we handcraft pure milk-based kulfis that rule hearts and make us the Kulfi King.

ROASTED ALMOND & FIG

The unique union of roasted almonds and figs is truly a match made in heaven. The smokey flavour of roasted almonds complements the sweetness of figs and creates a heavenly blend. This nutty kulfi is an ideal indulgence for every ceremonious occasion.

GUAVA

Made with the freshest and perfectly ripe guavas, our Guava kulfi offers you its real taste in every bite. A sprinkle of chilli powder adds to the sweet taste of this pink pleasure and makes it a dessert worth relishing.

GULBAHAR

In this world of standard flavours, Gulbahar serves as a breath of fresh air. This meadow of roses is our signature dessert which is loved even by the most discerning. Gulbahar is made with delicate rose petals, fresh gulkand, and a generous coating of dry fruits that make it the gold standard for kulfis.

KALKATTI PAAN

A lavish spread is only complete when it ends with a lush dessert. For centuries, paan has been eaten as a refreshing last course even in the royal courts. Our Kalkatti Paan kulfi, made with 100% natural paan ingredients, offers the same fragrant and freshening experience to your palates.

CHOCO ORANGE

Our Choco Orange kulfi is a melange of pure orange pulp and the finest chocolate coating. The tangy taste of orange combined with the richness of chocolate makes this tropical treat the best of both worlds.

CHOCO CHIPS

All that glitters is not gold. Likewise, all that is brown is not chocolate. Unlike other chocolate ice creams, our Choco Chips kulfi is made only with the pure and finest chocolate. The hidden treasure of crunchy choco chips and the rich chocolate coating make it a gourmet kulfi.

FRESH SITAFAL

With the arrival of the fall, sweet sitafals make an appearance in the market. Our Fresh Sitafal kulfi is a sinful saviour for those who love sitafal but loathe the tedious process of eating it. Having been made with freshly sourced custard apples, this fruity dessert offers the delight of ice cream with tender chunks of sitafal.
