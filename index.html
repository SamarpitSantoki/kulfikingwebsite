THE BRAND

What started as a home-based business in the year 1998, has today emerged as a King in the Kulfi segment. In this remarkable journey of 25 years, Kulfi King has made its presence not just in 18 states of India but even abroad. Our 100% natural ingredients and pure milk-based kulfis make us a much sought-after dessert brand, especially in the lavish event space.

CHOCO ORANGE

Our Choco Orange kulfi is a melange of pure orange pulp and the finest chocolate coating. The tangy taste of orange combined with the richness of chocolate makes this tropical treat the best of both worlds.

GULBAHAR

In this world of standard flavours, Gulbahar serves as a breath of fresh air. This meadow of roses is our signature dessert which is loved even by the most discerning. Gulbahar is made with delicate rose petals, fresh gulkand, and a generous coating of dry fruits that make it the gold standard for kulfis.
