ABOUT BRAND

In the year 1998, motivated by his parents, Mr. Kirti Acharya started an ice cream company from his home in Mumbai. Soon after the launch, his homemade kulfis caught on, and the business started to burgeon.

From making kulfis at home to having a full-fledged manufacturing setup in Anand, Gujarat, we have emerged as the Kulfi King. Today, the business is managed by Mr Rushi Acharya, the second generation entrepreneur, and under his astute leadership, we have made a presence in not just 18 states of India but even abroad.

Our 100% natural ingredients and pure milk-based kulfis make us a much sought-after dessert brand, especially in the lavish event space. In this remarkable journey of 25 years, we have served the likes of Aditya Birla and many more.
